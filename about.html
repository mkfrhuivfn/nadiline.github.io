Hello, I'm Nadi

A nature photographer based in the beautiful island of Phuket, Thailand — always chasing the perfect light.

I started my photography journey with a Nikon D5300 and a deep love for the natural world. From the golden coasts of Phuket to the lush forests of southern Thailand, I find beauty in every frame.

Photography, for me, is not just about taking pictures — it's about slowing down, observing, and finding the quiet poetry hidden in everyday nature.
